DAVA MEMBERSHIP ELIGIBILITY

Family and extended family members of veterans who were disabled or died in military service.


Family and extended family members of veterans who have served in the military without sustaining injury.


Family and extended family members of those in active military service.


Family and extended family of Disabled American Veterans Auxiliary members who are not otherwise eligible for membership.


Disabled American Veterans members.

DAVA DUES

Every person who is eligible should belong to the DAV Auxiliary if they believe in protecting the rights of disabled veterans and their families. By joining DAV Auxiliary, your support lends voice to our legislative efforts on behalf of all veterans and their families.


Life Membership - Life Membership is encouraged for those who want to be permanent members of the DAV Auxiliary. Members age 79 and under can pay their membership in one payment or start a life membership with a minimum $20 down payment and a flexible membership payment plan. Life Membership Life membership can be secured at any age with a $20 down payment and a flexible membership payment plan. This life membership seals your commitment to the Auxiliary, your veteran, and your family.

The DAV Auxiliary encourages junior member involvement in all programs of the organization. Learning by example, juniors experience first-hand the rewards of volunteerism and service to the community as well as the significance of honoring our nation's veterans.


New Junior Membership - Children age 17 and under have a complimentary membership until they change it to a Life Membership.


Junior Life Membership - Children age 17 and under can join the DAVA with a $20 down payment and a flexible membership payment plan.
