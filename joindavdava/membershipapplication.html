DAV MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION

Applicaions for Membership can be accomplished online at the

Disabled American Veterans National Website.

To Apply ONLINE, please go to

Click on "Membership", then "Join DAV|.

Applications for Membership can be made by mail to DAV National Headquarters.
Print the Membership Application Form shown in PDF form (see link below),
fill it in, and return with your payment to the address below:

Disabled American Veterans
National Headquarters
Post Office Box 145550
Cincinnati, Ohio 45250-5550

You can also apply directly for Membership to a local Chapter near you.
Please see our listing of Chapter Officers and Chapter locations in the Main Menu.

If you prefer, please give us a call at Disabled American Veterans, Department of Mississippi Headquarters
at (601)364-7178 and we will be pleased to help you in becoming a member of the DAVA.
